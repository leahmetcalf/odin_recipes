Delicious Vegan Cake

This is a recipe for a delicious vegan cake. A vanilla sponge with cream filling.

For this recipe you'll need to make sure that you have all the ingredients

You'll also need a kitchen.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • sugar
  • Plant-based milk
  • Baking powder
  • Vanilla extract

Method

  1. Pre-heat oven to 180 degrees
  2. Put the stuff in a bowl
  3. Mix it together
  4. Pour batter into a tin
  5. Bake for 20 minutes
